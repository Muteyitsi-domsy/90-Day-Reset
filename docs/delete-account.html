Delete Your Account

Renew90 - 90-Day Identity Reset App

We respect your right to delete your account and all associated data. You can do this directly in the app or by contacting us.

Option 1: Delete Within the App

  1. Open the Renew90 app
  2. Tap the Menu icon (three lines)
  3. Scroll to Data & Privacy
  4. Tap Delete All Data
  5. Confirm your choice when prompted

This will permanently delete:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All journal entries
  • All mood entries
  • All flip card entries
  • Your profile and settings
  • AI-generated reports and insights

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, email us to request account deletion:

Email: privacy@renew90.app

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your data is permanently removed from our production database immediately
  • Firebase backups are purged within 30 days
  • Audit logs (login history) are retained for 90 days for security compliance, then deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

Delete Specific Data Only

If you want to delete specific entries without deleting your entire account:

  1. Open any journal, mood, or flip entry
  2. Tap the delete icon (trash can)
  3. Confirm deletion

Contact Us

For questions about data deletion:

  • Privacy: privacy@renew90.app
  • Support: support@renew90.app
