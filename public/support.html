Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open the app, go to Menu → Data & Privacy → Delete All Data. This permanently removes your account and all associated journal entries.

How do I export my journal data?

Go to Menu → Data & Privacy → Download All Data to export your entries as a JSON file.

I forgot my PIN. How do I recover access?

On the PIN entry screen, tap Forgot PIN. A recovery code will be sent to your registered email address.

Is my journal data private?

Yes. If you use local storage (no account), your data never leaves your device. If you use cloud backup, your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell or share your journal content. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
